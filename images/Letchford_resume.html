Bio


Joe Letchford is a graphic designer from Richmond, Virginia. He specializes in graphic design, web design, art direction, visual identity systems, publications, and various print materials in the commercial and cultural sectors. Together with Josh Osborne, he runs the small enthusiastic non-geographic studio, Tiny Chime.


He believes strongly in experimentation and collaboration. His creative process involves looking beyond the specifications of each project to arrive at a meaningful solution through dialogue and close partnerships.
